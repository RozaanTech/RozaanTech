Who we are

RozaanTech builds purpose-driven automation for piping and plant design teams. We focus on delivering tools that integrate with your existing CAD and AVEVA setups (Hexagon too), so your engineers spend time on engineering — not repetitive clicks.

  • Custom macros & plugins for AutoCAD and AVEVA.
  • API integrations to automate data exchange and reports.
  • Workflow automation — from spool generation to revision control.
